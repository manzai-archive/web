# Notice & Takedown Policy

This archive contains user-contributed transcripts of publicly broadcast or
published comedy material, maintained for the purpose of language learning,
linguistic research, and comedy study.

**No audio or video files are hosted in this repository or on the site.**
Each entry links back to the original source on its hosting platform.

## DMCA / 著作権侵害申し立て / 版权投诉

If you are a rights holder (or authorized agent) and want material removed,
open an issue at
<https://github.com/manzai-archive/web/issues/new>
including:

1. Identification of the work (link to the entry on the site).
2. Statement of authority to act for the rights holder.
3. Contact email.

We aim to respond within 72 hours. Confirmed takedowns are added to
[`blocklist.yaml`](blocklist.yaml); the corresponding entry is excluded
from the next site build.

You may also file directly with GitHub:
<https://github.com/contact/dmca>

## Contributor responsibility

By submitting an entry you warrant that:

- It is an accurate transcript of publicly accessible material.
- The source URL points to the original (or an authorized) upload.
- You are not submitting material from private streams, leaked recordings,
  paywalled platforms, or otherwise non-public sources.
